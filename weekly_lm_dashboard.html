Key Insights & Actions Week of 23 Mar 2026
🚨Critical — Immediate Attention
Malaysia DSR dropped 3.8pp WoW to 89.1% — the biggest single-week decline across all markets. Also, % hubs doing 5 sweeps jumped from 7% to 87% — suggesting a sudden catch-up after prior non-compliance.
Singapore DSR remains at 66.1%, the lowest across all markets and declining further. Combined with SG's on-hold parcel rate of 6.1% (though improving), SG continues to need structural intervention.
Brazil loss rate worsened to 0.18% (+0.05pp WoW), driven by both hub and driver loss. Unreturned on-hold parcels at 22.6% — 10–20x higher than all other markets.
✅Positive Trends
Indonesia DSR improved +0.9pp to 90.4% — the only large market showing week-on-week improvement. Fleet productivity dipped slightly (-6) but loss rates held steady.
Brazil & Thailand DSR strong at 96.3% and 96.0% respectively. Thailand also leads in sweep compliance (97%) and expedite performance (97% in 3 days).
Expedite performance improving universally — all markets showing positive WoW for both B1 (1-day) and B2 (3-day) metrics. Indonesia showed biggest jump: +12pp for B1.
⚠️Watch Areas
Fleet productivity declining in 5 of 6 markets — VN dropped most (-12 parcels/driver), followed by ID (-6) and BR (-5). Only MY improved (+5). This may signal driver capacity or routing inefficiency.
ID WFM utilisation dropped 6pp to 84% — the only market tracking this metric that saw a decline. Worth monitoring if it persists next week.
Malaysia unreturned on-hold parcels rose +1.5pp to 3.4% — the only market deteriorating on this metric. Needs driver compliance follow-up.
🚀Feature Adoption Gaps
Smart Sorting adoption low in ID (31%) and VN (19%) despite being an Ops Compliance initiative. These markets should be prioritised for enforcement or training.
Smart Sequencing only at 34% in MY — and still "In Discussion" in TH and VN. Given its impact on fleet productivity, faster rollout could address the declining productivity trend.
MY leads on compliance features: Scan to Sort at 95%, Weekend at 93%. PH Weekend at 97% is the best performer. These are strong benchmarks for lagging markets.
